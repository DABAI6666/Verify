The 9/11 survival story of Michael Hingson and his dog-Truth! / 9/11 Attack on America , Animals , Terrorism / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 The Survival Story of Michael Hingson, led by his Guide Dog – Truth! Summary of eRumor: The escape of 51-year-old Michael Hingson who was on the 78th floor of the first tower to be hit by terrorist hijackers on September 11, 2001.  He was led to safety by his guide dog, Roselle. The Truth: This story has gotten quite a bit of visibility, including an interview on the Larry King show on CNN.  This particular version is actually a article from the Ventura County Star in California.
