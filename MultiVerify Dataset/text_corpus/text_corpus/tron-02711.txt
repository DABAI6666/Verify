Boeing test pilot tells it like it is to the Europeans-Fiction! / Space / Aviation , Tsunamis / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 Boeing Test Pilot “Tells It Like It Is” – Fiction! Summary of eRumor: A confrontation said to be between a Boeing test pilot and a group of Europeans who mocked the United States for sending an aircraft carrier to help tsunami victims. The Truth: Boeing test pilot Matt Archer tells TruthOrFiction.com that he did not have this conversation and that someone has been circulating the story with his name attached to it. Several websites on the Internet have published the story, some with Matt Archer’s name and some without any source. We have not confidently concluded what the origin of the story is.
