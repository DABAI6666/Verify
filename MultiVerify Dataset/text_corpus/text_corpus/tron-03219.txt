Alaskan Bush Pilot Views on Sarah Palin-Truth! / Politics / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 Alaskan Bush Pilot’s Personal Opinion Governor Sarah Palin- Truth! Summary of eRumor: The personal comments of a life time Alaskan  bush pilot named Butch King who is extremely proud of Governor Sarah Palin, calling her a “straight shooting, hard charging, get it done gal.”. The Truth: Butch King is the owner of the Wildman Lake Lodge on the Alaskan peninsula.   His website confirms that this email was written by him to a number of family members, friends and clients.   King did not intend for the email to get global recognition but has posted the email on his site granting permission to anyone wishing to forward it to others.
