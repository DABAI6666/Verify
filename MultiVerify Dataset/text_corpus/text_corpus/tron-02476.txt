An Urgent Call for Prayer from Anne Graham Lotz-Truth! / Miscellaneous , Religious / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 An Urgent Call for Prayer from Anne Graham Lotz- Truth! Summary of eRumor: This is an email allegedly sent by the daughter of evangelist Billy Graham, Anne Graham Lotz. It is an urgent call to prayer requesting that people pray for each of the first seven days in July and follow that with a 7 hour fast on July 7. The Truth: The prayer request is true and was found posted on the website of Ann Graham Lotz. Lotz also has a Facebook profile where she is writing about the 7 day event of prayer and fasting.
