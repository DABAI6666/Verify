Flaws in new U.S. quarters could be worth big bucks-Truth! / Promises / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 16, 2015 Turn Quarters into Dollars by Finding the Ones Minted With a Blemish- Truth! Summary of eRumor: A small number of quarters, nobody knows exactly how many, were minted with a feature upside down.   They are a part of the new “state series” and the ones with the flaws are designed to honor Pennsylvania, Delaware, and Connecticut. The Truth: This is true.  How do you tell whether you’ve got a defective one?  According to the U.S. Mint, Hold the quarter upright, head side facing you. Then flip it over top to bottom. On the defect, the tails side will appear upside down.  Normal quarters have the tail right side up.
