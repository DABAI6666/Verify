F-35 Airplane Takes Off Vertically, Flips-Fiction! / Space / Aviation / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 28, 2015 F-35 Airplane Takes Off Vertically, Flips- Fiction! Summary of eRumor: A viral video clip posted on YouTube allegedly shows an F-35 airplane take off vertically from an aircraft carrier and do a 360-degree flip before flying away. The Truth: This hoax shows footage taken from the video game Battlefield 2 . Similar videos featuring airplane tricks recorded by Battlefield 2 gamers are also posted on YouTube . Still, this hoax was based on an element of truth. Lockheed Martin’s F-35B Lightening does have vertical takeoff and landing capabilities. A video of what it looks like can be found here .
