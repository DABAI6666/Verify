Website That Offers a Search of U.S. Driver's Licenses-Fiction! / Humorous , Miscellaneous , Warnings / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 Website That Offers a Search of U.S. Driver’s Licenses- Fiction! Summary of eRumor: The email warns you that there is a site that has everybody’s driver’s license on it and you are warned that you should go to the site and have your information removed. The email directs you to a web page promoted as “Driver’s License Search.us.”  It claims to be a service of the National Motor Vehicle License Bureau. The Truth: This would be alarming if true but it’s a joke. If you use the page to search for anybody’s name, you’ll eventually be directed to a driver’s license with a picture of a baboon on it!
