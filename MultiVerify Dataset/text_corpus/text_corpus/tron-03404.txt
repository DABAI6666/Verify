Chaplian's Report From Bombing in Mosul, Iraq-Truth! / Religious , War / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 First-hand Report From a Chaplain in Iraq about the Suicide Bombing in an Army Mess Hall in December, 2004 – Truth! Summary of eRumor: A long, personal narrative from a chaplain in Iraq who went to the scene of the bombing of a mess hall filled with U.S. soldiers and local workers on December 21, 2004.  Twenty-two people were killed and dozens of others injured, mostly Americans. The Truth: The email is authentic and was written by Army Chaplain Brad Lewis who served at the high security Army base in Mosul.
