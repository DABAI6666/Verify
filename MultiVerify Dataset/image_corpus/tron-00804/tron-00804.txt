Clint Eastwood Saved Life of Choking Man with Heimlich-Truth! / Celebrities / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 Clint Eastwood Saved Life of Choking Man with Heimlich- Truth! Summary of eRumor: This is a forwarded email that contains what appears to be a news article that alleged that actor Clint Eastwood saved the life of a man who was choking at a party at a PGA tournament. The Truth: Clint Eastwood saved the life of choking tournament director Steve John with a Heimlich maneuver while they were attending a PGA function at at the Monterey Conference Center.   This according to a February 9, 2014 article by ESPN , the source of the forwarded news article.
