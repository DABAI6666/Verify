Dollar Bills Circulating with "No God But Allah" Stamped on Their Backs-Unproven! / Religious , Warnings / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 Dollar Bills Circulating with “No God But Allah” Stamped on Their Backs- Unproven! Summary of eRumor: This is a story about an unnamed woman from Monte Vista, Colorado who found a one dollar bill that had the words “No God But Allah” stamped on the back of the bill just under the words “In God We Trust.”   The email includes a photo of the bill.
