Summary of eRumor: The email is an article said to be written by Country Western Singer Charlie Daniels about his visit with U.S. troops stationed at Guantanamo Bay, Cuba.  He describes the treatment of Taliban prisoners being held there and says they are probably better off than they’ve ever been in terms of nutrition and healthcare.  He says that is in stark contrast with the way the prisoners treat their captors.  He says the media are not reporting the truth about all that is happening there and is critical of attorneys who would take up the cause of the prisoners.
