Menu Toggle About Home Pleas Help The Lopez Family-Truth! Help The Lopez Family-Truth! / Pleas / By Rich Buhler & Staff / March 17, 2015 A Plea for help for the Lopez Family- Truth! Summary of eRumor: A forwarded email that tells a story of a series of tragic events in the life of a family in Melrose Park, Illinois and asks for donations. The Truth: The plea for help is real and began circulating in December of 2008. The email originated from a woman named Dorlelia Riveria who functioned as an advocated for the Lopez Family and confirmed with Truthorfiction.com that she has set up a fund at the MB Financial Bank in Chicago, Illinois for donations for the Lopez family. This family has been met with a series of tragic challenges from the unemployment of Sergio Lopez, the death of his wife San Juana Lopez, and a fire that destroyed the home that they were renting.  Their son, Brian, was born with cerebral palsy and has been experiencing health problems since the death of his mother.
